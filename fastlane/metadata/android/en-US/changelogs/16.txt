### What's New in Version 1.0.6

**Step History Reliability**
- Fixed daily step history backfill so missed days are restored the next time you open the app.
- Improved same-day step sync so opening later in the day shows the full total from midnight.

**Workout Save Reliability**
- Completed workouts now save immediately at session end, reducing the chance of losing a result before leaving the summary screen.

**Health Sync Stability**
- Improved startup timing for Health sync so daily step updates initialize more reliably after app launch.
