### What's New in Version 1.0.2

**Daily Step Tracking & Goals**
- **Match Ready Tracker**: Your phone now tracks your daily steps directly on the Dashboard. Hit your daily goal to earn the "MATCH READY" badge.
- Enhanced step counter accuracy and performance with seamless midnight resets and detailed history logs.

**Live Football Scores**
- **Faster, Accurate Match Data**: We've upgraded the Livescore engine to pull real-time football match data instantly, ensuring highly accurate scores and lightning-fast load times. 

**Elite HUD Redesign & Quicker Workouts**
- **Sleek Workout HUD**: The central progress ring has been completely redesigned with a split-zone layout, boldly displaying your Lap Timer on top and Target BPM on the bottom.
- **Dynamic Laps**: The green tracking ring now corresponds exactly to your active lap or interval duration. 
- **Faster Recovery**: Reduced maximum rest periods to **45 seconds** across all Barbell, Dumbbell, and Bench presets to keep your heart rate up and enforce an elite training pace.

**Bug Fixes & Improvements**
- Improved reliability for Google Sign-In authentication.
- Fixed a bug causing the "Connect Bike" button to linger post-workout.
- Improved iOS support and general UI performance optimizations.
